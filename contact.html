Frequently Asked Questions

Is NamiQuiz free to use?

Yes! NamiQuiz is completely free. No registration or payment is required. Just visit the website and start learning!

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required. You can optionally enter your name to track your scores, but it's not mandatory. We prioritize easy access for all learners.

Can I retake quizzes?

Absolutely! You can take any quiz as many times as you want. Questions are randomized each time, so every attempt is a new learning opportunity.

Can teachers use this in classrooms?

Yes! Teachers are welcome to use NamiQuiz as a supplementary teaching tool or for assessments. We encourage educational use in all forms.

Will more categories be added?

We're constantly working to expand our question bank and add new categories. If you have suggestions for topics, please contact us!

How can I contribute questions?

We welcome contributions from educators and experts! Send us your questions via email at feedback@namiquiz.na, and we'll review them for inclusion.
