Frequently Asked Questions

Is NamiQuiz free to use?

Yes! NamiQuiz is completely free. Create an account to track your progress or use as a guest!

Do I need to create an account?

No! You can take quizzes as a guest. However, creating an account lets you save progress, track scores, and resume quizzes later.

Can I retake quizzes?

Absolutely! You can take any quiz as many times as you want. Questions are randomized each time for a fresh learning experience.

Can teachers use this in classrooms?

Yes! Teachers are welcome to use NamiQuiz as a supplementary teaching tool or for assessments. We encourage educational use!
